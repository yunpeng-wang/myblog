About The Site

Updated: June 21, 2025

Where current began to flow.

👋👋 Hi there! I’m a Chinese passionate wildlife photography enthusiast who finds joy — and sometimes, sheer awe — in capturing fleeting moments of nature through the lens. On the other hand, I’m an engineer in the automotive industry, currently working in Japan — guess I like solving things, whether it’s light or logic.

This blog is my little corner of the internet where I document those precious encounters. It’s part photo diary, part long-term journal — meant not just to showcase images, but to preserve the stories and subtleties that often lie beyond the frame.

The idea for creating this space was, in part, born out of frustration with ever-changing policies on photo-sharing platforms (yes, looking at you, Flickr and Google Photos! ). I wanted a place where my work could live more securely, on my own terms.

But more than that, I believe there’s something elegant — maybe even poetic — about keeping a dedicated log of one’s wildlife photography journey. After all, a single image can be beautiful, but the memories, the patience, and the quiet thrill behind each shot deserve to be told too.

Also… let’s be honest — having my own domain and a space that’s fully mine? That just feels pretty darn cool.
